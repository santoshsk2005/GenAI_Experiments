What The Agents Do

Each agent emulates a human rater: it opens a saved quote page for its insurer, extracts coverage baselines, and adjusts them using underwriting signals such as age, credit, mileage, and loyalty. No APIs are called—only controlled HTML snapshots are parsed to mimic on-site data collection.
